This is the pre-launch survey for fleebmarket. Fleebmarket is a would-be keyboard and keyboard parts second hand market.

I am running this survey in order to have a little more insight into the subject, and have some more guidance on what might be most desired features. I will launch Fleebmarket whatever the results, and, depending on the traction it gets, will keep on with the development of new features or not.

I do intend to make some money off of it eventually, but I am mostly interested in providing a nice experience and designing the right tool for the job.

You can read more about the Fleebarket project in the about page.

Since log in is not required to take the survey, it is possible to submit multiple results. Please restrain yourself from doing so :)
